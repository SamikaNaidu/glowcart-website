About GlowCart

GlowCart is a modern beauty and fashion shopping platform created to make stylish and quality products accessible to everyone. From makeup and skincare to fashion and fragrances, we bring everything together in one place.

Our goal is to provide a simple, smooth, and enjoyable shopping experience. We focus on trendy products, affordable pricing, and customer satisfaction.

This website is a frontend project built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript as part of a Web Technology assignment. It demonstrates multipage navigation, reusable components like header and footer, and basic interactivity.

Our Mission

To deliver trendy, affordable, and high-quality beauty and fashion products to customers with a great online experience.

Our Vision

To become a trusted and loved online destination for beauty and fashion shopping.
